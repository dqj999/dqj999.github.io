Collecting and Using Your Personal Data

Types of Data Collected

Personal Data

While using Our Service, We may ask You to provide Us with the following personally identifiable information that can be used to contact or identify You.

  • A nickname for each Account you create

  • A local authority name for each Account you create

  • Your library card number for each Account you create

Other Data

  • Information regarding your location

    • If you use the Nearest Libraries option and you select the current location Icon you will be asked for your consent to access your accurate location

    This information is used to provide details of libraries that are near to your stated or detected location.

    You can enable or disable access to this information at any time, through Your Device settings.

Use of Your Personal Data

We use the Personal Data for the following purposes:

  • To access your enrolled accounts in your selected Libraries

    The access is performed using a Webview to connect to your selected account with the Library.

Retention of Your Personal Data

We will retain Your Personal Data only for as long as is necessary for the purposes set out in this Privacy Policy.

You can remove the data by removing the Account details from the Accounts screen.

Security of Your Personal Data

Your Personal Data is held in encrypted form on your Device and is only transmitted in encrypted form to the Service Provider

Children's Privacy

Our Service does not address anyone under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personally identifiable information from anyone under the age of 13. If You are a parent or guardian and You are aware that Your child has provided the Application with Personal Data, please contact us at the link below. If we become aware that the Application has collected Personal Data from anyone under the age of 13 without verification of parental consent, we will take steps to remove that information from our Appplication.
