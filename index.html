Mylib for UK Libraries Privacy Policy

Last updated: 10 January 2023

Collecting and Using Your Personal Data

Personal Data

When setting up access to your selected library accounts we ask you to provide the application with the following personally identifiable information that can be used to contact or identify you.

  • A nickname for each account access that you create

  • The local authority name of the library for each account access that you create

  • Your library card number for each account access that you create

Other Data

  • Information regarding your location

    • If you use the Nearest Libraries option you will be asked for your consent to access your accurate location

    This information is used to provide details of libraries that are near to your stated or detected location.

    You can enable or disable access to this information at any time, through Your Device settings.

Use of Your Personal Data

We use the Personal Data for the following purposes:

  • To access your enrolled accounts in your selected Libraries

    The access is performed using a Webview to connect to your selected account with the Library.

Retention of Your Personal Data

We will retain Your Personal Data only for as long as is necessary for the application to access your selected accounts.

You can remove the data by removing the account details from the Accounts screen.

Security of Your Personal Data

Your Personal Data is held in encrypted form on your Device and is only transmitted in encrypted form to the selected library's Service Provider

Children's Privacy

Our Service does not address anyone under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personally identifiable information from anyone under the age of 13. If you are a parent or guardian and you are aware that your child has provided the Application with Personal Data, please contact us at the link below. If we become aware that the Application has collected Personal Data from anyone under the age of 13 without verification of parental consent, we will take steps to remove that information from our Application.

Links to Other Websites

Our Service may contain links to other websites that are not operated by us. The Application may use an Information Service Provider Website to provide part of the Service to you. These have their own Privacy Policies which apply in addition to those of the Application. If you follow a link to one of your selected accounts or you follow a third party link, you will be directed to that third party's site. We strongly advise You to review the Privacy Policy of every site You visit.

We have no control over and assume no responsibility for the content, privacy policies or practices of any third party sites or services.

Changes to this Privacy Policy

We may update Our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify You of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page.

You are advised to review this Privacy Policy periodically for any changes. Changes to this Privacy Policy are effective when they are posted on this page.

Contact Us

If you have any questions or issues about this Privacy Policy, please contact the developer:
